To start a game of set, follow these steps.

  1. Pick one person to create the room. That player will do steps 2-3 while the others wait until step 4 to do anything.

  2. The player setting up the game should follow this link and then the "new private game" button.

  3. Copy the invite URL and share it with the other players using the Gather chat. Then click the "Start Game" button.

  4. Once the URL has been shared, click here and enter the URL when prompted.
