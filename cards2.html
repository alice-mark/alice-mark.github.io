For a deck of cards, follow these steps.

  1. Pick one player to set up the game. That player will do steps 2 and 3 while the others wait until step 4 to do anything.

  2. The player setting up the game should follow this link and then click the "Start Game" button.

  3. Before clicking "ENTER", copy the URL and share it with the other players using the Gather chat.

  4. Once the URL has been shared, everyone else should click here and enter the URL when prompted.
