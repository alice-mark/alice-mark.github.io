To start a game of Codenames, follow these steps.

  1. Pick one player to set up the game. That player will do steps 2 and 3 while the others wait until step 4 to do anything.

  2. The player setting up the game should follow this link and then click the button. Set up the room by choosing the language and your nickname. Once you are in the room, click the botton on the top left that looks like this: to get a share URL.

  3. Copy the URL and share it with the other players using the Gather chat.

  4. Once the URL has been shared, click here and enter the URL when prompted.
