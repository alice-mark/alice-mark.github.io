To start a collaborative jigsaw puzzle, follow these steps.

  1. Pick one person to set up the puzzle. That player will do steps 2 and 3 while the others wait until step 4 to do anything.

  2. The player setting up the game should follow this link and then browse around to find a puzzle to do. Once you've chosen a puzzle, click the "Play this Puzzle" button. When the puzzle opens you'll see a settings menu in the center of the screen. Click on the people button:
    Then click the "Create Game Link" button in the side pane.

  3. Copy the URL and share it with the other players using the Gather chat.

  4. Once the URL has been shared, everyone should click here and enter the URL when prompted. The person who set up the puzzle will need to go back to the gather room and press x again to enter the link.
